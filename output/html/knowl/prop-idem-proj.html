Proposition 1.2.2.

Let \(E\) be a non-zero idempotent on \(\hilbert\text{.}\) The following are equivalent:

  1. \(E\) is a projection.

  2. \(E\) is the orthogonal projection of \(\hilbert\) onto \(\ran E\text{.}\)

  3. \(\norm{E} = 1\text{.}\)

  4. \(E\) is self-adjoint.

  5. \(E\) is normal.

  6. \(\ip{Eh}{h} \geq 0\) for all \(h \in \hilbert\text{.}\)
