Let \(\hilbert\) be a finite dimensional Hilbert space, and \(V \subset \hilbert\) a linear subspace. Let \(\{v_1, \ldots, v_k\}\) be an orthonormal basis for \(V\text{.}\) The projection operator of \(\hilbert\) onto \(V\), denoted \(P_V\text{,}\) is given by

\begin{equation*} P_V h = \sum_{i=1}^k \ip{h}{v_i} v_i \in V \end{equation*}
