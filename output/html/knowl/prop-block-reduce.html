Proposition 1.2.5.

Suppose that \(A \in \BH\text{,}\) \(\M\) is a closed linear subspace of \(\hilbert\) and that \(P = P_{\M}\text{.}\) The following are equivalent.

  1. \(\M\) is invariant for \(A\text{.}\)

  2. \(PAP = AP\text{.}\)

  3. The block \(Z\) as in the block representation above is the \(0\) operator.

The following statemens are also equivalent.

  1. \(\M\) reduces \(A\text{.}\)

  2. \(PA = AP\text{.}\)

  3. In the block representation above, both \(W, Z\) are \(0\) operators.

  4. \(\M\) is invariant for both \(A\) and \(A\ad\text{.}\)
