Definition 1.2.4.

If \(A \in \BH\) and \(\M\) is a closed linear subspace of \(\hilbert\text{,}\) then we say \(\M\) is an invariant subspace of \(A\) if \(A\M \subseteq \M\text{.}\) If in addition \(A \M^\perp \subseteq \M^\perp\text{,}\) we say that \(\M\) reduces \(A\) or that \(\M\) is a reducing subspace of \(A\text{.}\)
