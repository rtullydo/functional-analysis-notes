Suppose a family of closed sets \(\{F_\alpha\}\) is given. Let \(\{U_\alpha\}\) be the family of open sets given by \(U_\alpha = (F_\alpha)^c\text{.}\) By construction, \(\bigcap_{\alpha \in \A} F_\alpha \neq \emptyset\) if and only if \(\bigcup_{\alpha \in \A} U_\alpha \neq \X\text{.}\) Also, \(\{F_\alpha\}\) has the finite intersection property if and only if no finite subfamily of \(\{U_\alpha\}\) covers \(\X\text{.}\)
