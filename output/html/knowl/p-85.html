One useful characterization of compactness is in terms of closed sets. A family of sets \(\{F_\alpha\}_{\alpha \in \mathcal A}\) has the finite intersection property if \(\bigcap_{\alpha \in \mathcal B} \neq \emptyset\) for any finite subset \(\mathcal B\) of \(\mathcal A\text{.}\)
